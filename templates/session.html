Session Summary

Ask the Council

How does this work?

The Council gives you 5 different expert perspectives on any question:

  • The Contrarian looks for what could go wrong or what you might be overlooking
  • The First Principles Thinker strips away assumptions and asks what you're really trying to solve
  • The Expansionist finds opportunities and upside you might be missing
  • The Outsider sees your situation with completely fresh eyes, no insider bias
  • The Executor focuses on what you should actually do next, concretely

After all 5 respond independently, they review each other's blind spots. Then a synthesis pulls everything together into a clear recommendation with one concrete next step. The whole process takes about 30-60 seconds.

About Nudges

Nudges are friendly reminders you can send to participants to let them know the conversation is waiting for their input.

What they receive

Subject: [Your name] is waiting for you on Vilora

"Hey [name], your conversation is waiting. [Your name] wanted to check in. Your conversation about [topic] has new activity and they'd love to hear your thoughts. No pressure. Just a friendly reminder that the conversation is there whenever you're ready."

Limits

  • You can send up to 4 email nudges per person per session
  • There's a 24-hour cooldown between email nudges to the same person
  • Text nudges (mobile only) open your messaging app and don't count toward the limit

Tips for nudging

  • Give people time to respond before nudging. Not everyone checks email right away
  • If someone hasn't responded after 2-3 nudges, consider reaching out personally
  • Nudges work best when spaced out. A daily nudge feels persistent; an hourly one feels pushy
  • Remember that some people may need time to think before joining a conversation
